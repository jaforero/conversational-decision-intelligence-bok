The foundational core defines what the CDI-BoK is, where CDI begins and ends, what language it controls and how it organizes knowledge. Javier Forero ratified its five central assets on July 21, 2026 for stable release `v0.4.0`.

## Growth principle

The taxonomy does not require chapters to fill boxes. First identify a decision need; then determine which domain owns the concept, what evidence exists and what minimum asset adds value without duplicating knowledge.

!!! warning "Calibrated authority"
    Publishing this core does not prove CDI is a consolidated academic discipline. The Constitution and definitions have institutional authority within the project; empirical claims retain an independent evidence assessment.
