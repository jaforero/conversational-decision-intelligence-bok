# About the CDI-BoK

The **Conversational Decision Intelligence Body of Knowledge** is an initiative founded and led by **Javier Forero**, a statistician and specialist in analytics, data science, artificial intelligence and decision transformation.

## Purpose

Build a common language and knowledge architecture so organizations and professionals can design Human + AI collaboration for better decisions—not merely produce more reports, dashboards or automation.

## Transparency principles

- The project distinguishes its mission from recognition already achieved.
- PULSE retains identifiable constitutional documents.
- AI-assisted sources are not presented as primary evidence.
- Original contributions state their comparison, limits and validation status.
- The public repository preserves decisions, versions and controls.
