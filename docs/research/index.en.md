# Research

The CDI-BoK does not use “Deep Research” as a synonym for validated evidence. AI-assisted syntheses can locate concepts, tensions and candidate references; every material source must be checked against the original and assessed for quality, recency and relevance.

## Corpus status

| Layer | Permitted use | Current limit |
|---|---|---|
| PULSE constitutional sources | Govern PULSE identity and doctrine | Institutional authority, not empirical validation |
| Javier Forero's original points of view | Trace authorship and proposed contributions | Some available documents are incomplete |
| AI-assisted Deep Research | Map the state of the art and candidate bibliography | Does not replace systematic review or reading originals |
| Scientific and industry literature | Support or challenge claims | Must be incorporated with explicit quality assessment |

## Immediate agenda

- delimit CDI against Decision Intelligence, Conversational Analytics, DSS and Human-Centered AI;
- test the originality of PULSE and its components;
- build a taxonomy of claims and validation designs;
- instrument cases that connect decision, action, result and learning;
- investigate when copilots and agents improve or impair decisions.

**Central risk:** confusing novelty of language with theoretical novelty, and theoretical novelty with demonstrated effectiveness.
