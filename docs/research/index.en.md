# Research

<span class="cdi-release">Evidence Backbone · Candidate v0.9.0-rc.1</span>

The CDI-BoK does not use “Deep Research” as a synonym for validated evidence.
AI-assisted syntheses can locate concepts, tensions and candidate references;
every material source must be checked against its original and assessed against
the claim it is intended to support.

## Start with your question

| If you need to… | Route | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| understand DI and the maturity of the field | [DI state of the art](decision-intelligence-state-of-the-art.md) | foundations, boundaries, disputes and limits |
| know what authority each PULSE component has | [PULSE evidence map](pulse-evidence-map.md) | constitutional, derived, candidate, hypothesis or unevaluated |
| use trends without treating them as facts | [Future signals](future-signals.md) | scenarios, horizons and update conditions |
| decide what to study next | [Research agenda](research-agenda.md) | falsifiable questions and minimum designs |

## How knowledge is organized

| Layer | Examples | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Established foundations | Decision Analysis, DSS, information quality, outcome bias | primary or official source and application boundaries |
| Mature evolving practices | DMN, EDM, data governance and AI risk | version and `as_of` date |
| Emerging fields | human–AI deliberation, conversational analytics and agents | context-dependent evidence |
| PULSE synthesis | PDAMR, Decision Circle, five verbs and Human-in-Control | project authority, not independent effectiveness |
| Hypotheses and metaphors | allostasis, interoception and decision organism | observable prediction and abandonment possibility |
| Future signals | expected changes in interfaces, agents and governance | scenario, not fact or readiness |

## Verifiable backbone

The `governance/registries/evidence-profiles.yml` registry preserves hashes,
provenance, completeness, rights, permitted use and eight dimensions without
turning them into one total score. `ADR-028` blocks incomplete sources,
AI-assisted syntheses as direct support and forecasts used as facts.

<div class="cdi-risk" markdown>
**Central risk:** confusing novelty of language with theoretical novelty, and
theoretical novelty with demonstrated effectiveness.
