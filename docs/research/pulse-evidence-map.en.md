# PULSE evidence map

This map answers one concrete question: **what authority does each PULSE
component have today, and what does it not authorize us to claim?** It does not
produce a total score or turn abundant documentation into validation.

| Component | Status | Admissible support | Decisive boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| PULSE identity and definition | Constitutional | PULSE DNA and Identity | Owner authority, not empirical effectiveness |
| Decision First and PDAMR | Constitutional and practice | DNA, Identity and complete author POV | No external performance comparison exists |
| Decision Circle | Constitutional | DNA and Identity | The cycle organizes practice; it does not prove learning |
| Five verbs | Practice synthesis | Author POV and constitutional sources | They are not a universal sequence |
| Human-in-Control | Constitutional principle | DNA, Identity and BoK controls | Documenting it does not guarantee effective control |
| Decision Experience | Candidate contract | Derived architecture and specification | Requires evaluation with real users and tasks |
| L0–L7 architecture | Candidate derived architecture | `SRC-ARCH-001` | Not approved doctrine or a validated architecture |
| Five levels of analytics | Author pedagogical framework | `SRC-JF-006` | Not a universal maturity scale |
| Interoception, allostasis and decision organism | Metaphors and hypotheses | Derived architecture | Not causal mechanisms or biological equivalence |
| Longitudinal record | Learning hypothesis | DNA, architecture and Measurement Record | Recording does not prove behavior or outcome change |
| B2B case | Instrumented, unexecuted design | MVP design and simulated data | No real action, observed outcome or attribution |

## Claim compatibility

- Constitutional sources may govern PULSE definitions.
- An author POV may explain lineage or purpose.
- A derived architecture may support a candidate design hypothesis.
- None of those classes can establish superiority or effectiveness by itself.
- `SRC-JF-001` and `SRC-JF-003` remain blocked because their copies are
  truncated.
- An AI-assisted synthesis does not replace an original source or validate the
  architecture.

## Open tensions

### Practical framework versus technology ladder

The five-level framework may help teach analytics capabilities, but it conflicts
with Decision First if interpreted as a requirement to “move up” before
defining the decision. It remains a pedagogical taxonomy, not a mandatory
maturity path.

### Biological metaphor versus causal explanation

Interoception and allostasis can generate useful questions about signals,
feedback and adaptation. The risk is turning verbal similarity into a
mechanism. Any derived prediction must be observable without requiring the
metaphor to be literally true.

### Integration versus originality

PULSE may create value through selection and coordination even when many
components have antecedents. Assembly originality, theoretical novelty and
effectiveness are separate claims requiring separate evidence.

## Next evidence threshold

Before an effectiveness claim is strengthened, a pilot needs:

1. a decision, population and comparator defined in advance;
2. a verifiable baseline and evidence quality;
3. operational human authority and stop conditions;
4. outcome, process, adoption and risk measured separately;
5. enough follow-up to observe action and learning;
6. publication of adverse, null and cost results.
