# Release v0.4.0

`v0.4.0` is the first stable release of the CDI-BoK foundational core. It promotes `v0.4.0-rc.1` after Javier Forero's explicit ratification on July 21, 2026.

## Promoted authority

| Asset | Status | Authority | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| CDI-BoK Constitution | Approved | Foundational | Governs project purpose, principles and evolution |
| CDI scope and boundaries | Approved | Institutional | Controls the project definition and limits |
| Glossary | Approved | Controlled | Sets the initial vocabulary without taking concepts owned by PULSE |
| Domain map | Approved | Controlled | Organizes eight areas and 29 domains as an editorial taxonomy |
| PULSE specification | Approved | Derived | Projects constitutional sources; does not replace them |

## What “stable” does not mean

Stability describes project governance and publishing status. It does not prove that CDI is a consolidated scientific discipline, the CDI-BoK is a recognized standard, the term CDI originated here, or PULSE has generalizable effectiveness. Research, Practice Lab and assets outside the ratification retain their prior status.

## Closure evidence

- Ratification governed by ADR-017.
- Reproducible editorial and technical preflight.
- Strict portal build.
- Visual regression against canonical Ubuntu runner baselines.
- Automated Axe rules without violations in the defined scope.

Automated results alone do not constitute a WCAG 2.2 AA conformance claim.
