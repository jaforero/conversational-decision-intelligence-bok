# Release v0.8.0

`v0.8.0` establishes a **stable editorial and technical baseline** after ratification of `v0.8.0-rc.1`. It integrates the foundational core, learning path, measurement system, Practice Lab and conversational patterns under reproducible navigation, traceability and gates.

## What becomes stable

- the MkDocs portal and its public architecture;
- navigation from decision and evidence to action, measurement and learning;
- strict build, semantic audit, visual regression and automated accessibility contracts;
- identification of authority, sources, claims, limits and asset status;
- version lineage from `v0.2.0` through source candidate `v0.8.0-rc.1`;
- the mechanism that creates a tag and GitHub Release only from the validated `main` SHA.

## Authority preserved by component

| Component | Retained status | What this release does not do |
|---|---|---|
| Governance `v0.2.0` | Approved and project-normative | Does not make CDI-BoK an external standard |
| Core `v0.4.0` | Approved with differentiated authority | Does not change definitions or scope |
| B2B case `v0.5.0-rc.1` | Candidate, instrumented, unexecuted | Does not invent owner, action, outcome or effect |
| Learning `v0.6.0-rc.1` | Ratified candidate guidance | Does not prove real learning or adoption |
| Measurement `v0.7.0-rc.1` | Ratified candidate synthesis | Does not validate weights, benchmarks or a universal score |
| Patterns `v0.8.0-rc.1` | Ratified candidate taxonomy | Does not make it exhaustive, scientific or effective by decree |

Stability applies to the **publishing system and integrated baseline**, not to uniformly promoting every document to `approved`.

## Promotion gates

The RC merged through PR #10 as `bbc5c0583b28bbc3d4d2b6a2aedf28f8df336347`. Post-merge validation `29871891960` and deployment `29871892015` passed; the portal returned HTTP 200. The promotion PR and post-merge stable workflow repeated preflight, build, browser and Axe gates before creating `v0.8.0` on the exact validated SHA.

## What “stable” does not mean

CDI remains a proposed interdisciplinary domain; PULSE is not presented as scientifically validated or guaranteed to improve decisions; the pattern taxonomy is not academic consensus; the B2B case uses simulated data without observed impact; automated audits do not prove complete WCAG 2.2 AA conformance; and `0.x` still denotes foundational development.

## Immutable identification

ADR-024 authorizes the `v0.8.0` tag and GitHub Release. Both are created only after merge and stable gates; `v0.8.0-rc.1` remains untagged under ADR-022.
