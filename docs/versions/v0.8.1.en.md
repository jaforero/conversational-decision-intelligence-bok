# Release v0.8.1

`v0.8.1` establishes the **ES/EN bilingual portal as a stable editorial and technical baseline**. It promotes `v0.8.1-rc.1` after verifying 46 content pairs, localized navigation and interface, equivalent routes, SEO metadata and automated translation-drift controls.

## Stable scope

- canonical Spanish at historical routes and complete English under `/en/`;
- a contextual selector that preserves the equivalent page;
- localized navigation, search, interface, announcements, footer and social metadata;
- canonical, ES/EN `hreflang` and `x-default`;
- a translation registry with version, status, owner, review date and source hash;
- strict build, semantic audit, Playwright, visual regression and Axe.

## Lineage

PR #12 implemented the bilingual candidate and merged as `655e3b80ad1cbd8b443b6339358996f9fe656083`. PR #13 removed Spanish interface residue from the English edition and merged as `5837657f6bfe0627f5bd0c8ada13640dba9a0a4b`. Post-hotfix validation `29882684997`, deployment `29882684960` and public HTTP 200 verification closed the candidate before this promotion.

## Boundaries

- English is a governed translation and does not create co-canonical doctrine.
- The `v0.4.0` core retains its approved authority.
- Learning, measurement and patterns retain candidate maturity.
- The B2B case remains instrumented and unexecuted.
- Portal stability does not scientifically validate CDI or PULSE or demonstrate effectiveness or impact.
- Automated checks do not establish complete WCAG 2.2 AA conformance.

ADR-026 authorizes the `v0.8.1` tag and GitHub Release only on the `main` SHA that passes the post-merge stable gates.
