# Release v0.8.2

`v0.8.2` makes the post-`v0.8.1` experience fixes a stable baseline. The Spanish edition localizes its reader-facing labels, the home page explains the business outcome more clearly and search strictly follows the selected language.

## What changes

- Localized Spanish navigation and titles, with the controlled English term at first mention.
- `Enlace permanente` tooltip in ES and `Permanent link` in EN.
- A home value proposition focused on better business decisions.
- Separate ES/EN indexes, each with 47 root pages and no routes from the other language.
- Automated controls against localization regressions and mixed-language search.

## What does not change

- Approved authority remains limited to the `v0.4.0` core and applicable controlled records.
- Learning, measurement and patterns remain candidates.
- PULSE is not presented as scientifically validated.
- The B2B case remains instrumented and not executed.
- A stable build does not demonstrate organizational impact.

ADR-027 authorizes the `v0.8.2` tag and GitHub Release only on the `main` SHA that passes every post-merge gate.
