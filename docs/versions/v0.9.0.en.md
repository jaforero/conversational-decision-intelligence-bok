# Release v0.9.0

`v0.9.0` promotes the ratified `v0.9.0-rc.1` candidate to a stable
publication baseline. It freezes a reproducible system for profiling sources,
matching claims to compatible evidence and publishing the Decision Intelligence
state of the art, PULSE evidence map, future signals and research agenda in
Spanish and English.

## What becomes stable

- The evidence-profile schema and its usage boundaries.
- Traceability among sources, claims, counterevidence and uncertainty.
- Executable separation among primary sources, discovery aids, incomplete documents and prospective signals.
- Bilingual Research publication and controlled navigation.
- Editorial, technical, visual and automated accessibility gates.

## What is not elevated

- The thesis of DI as an emerging integrative field remains provisional.
- PULSE remains a project synthesis; it is not scientifically validated.
- Hypotheses, metaphors, patterns and recommendations retain their individual maturity.
- A stable bundle does not demonstrate effectiveness, adoption, causality or organizational impact.
- Automated checks do not establish full WCAG 2.2 AA conformance.

ADR-029 authorizes the `v0.9.0` tag and GitHub Release only on the `main` SHA
that contains this promotion and passes every post-merge gate.
